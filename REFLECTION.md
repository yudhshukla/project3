### Describe your process. At a high level, how did you go about creating this project?
- I asked Claude what was feasible to implement given the scope of this project (8 hours, AI-guided) from SimCity. I kept a list of features I wanted to include and the order in which I'd add them. I decided I'd add the database and frontend-backend communication at the end since I wanted to focus on the gameplay, which might not have been ideal, but I think it worked out fine. For each step, I either asked AI to implement it and explain it or to teach me how to implement it by giving me function headers, step-by-step guided approaches, and comments. 


### What AI tools and strategies did you use? What models did you choose, and were you using them in a browser chat, in something like Cursor, in the command line, etc? Did you use an agentic process like in HW8, or something else?
- I used Claude and Gemini. I used Claude in the command line and Gemini in my browser. I did not use an agentic approach, but a more conversational/teaching one.  

### Why did you make the choices above?
- I didn't go for an agentic approach since I wanted to make sure I understood when/where/why each change was being made. I also didn't want to overuse AI. 


### What changed from your pre-113 approach? Compare your approach to how you might have approached the project before 15-113. What has changed, if anything? Do you feel more or less well-equipped to create projects like this within a reasonable time frame?
- Giving the AI agent one step at a time, and guiding it/providing it only the relevant code to work with. I also think I have a better understanding of which agents can do well and what they can't do well. For example, since I have Gemini Pro, it is good at making visual changes I want since it can process the images I upload better than other models. Meanwhile for changes that affect multiple files, I know that a command-line tool would be more effective. I feel more equipped in terms of time management and scoping; I have a better understanding of what projects are feasible to complete in x amount of time with the help of AI.
